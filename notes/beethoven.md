# Ludwig van Beethoven

## Historical Context
Late Classical and early Romantic visionary who composed while battling profound deafness.

## Main Characteristics
- Transformed music into public, philosophical art exploring profound human struggle.
- Expanded orchestral size, duration, and dynamic boundaries significantly.
- Pioneered the idea of the independent, highly respected freelance artist

## Learning Insights
Personal struggles and limitations can be channeled to create universally profound art.

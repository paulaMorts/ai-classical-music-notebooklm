# Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

## Historical Context
Austrian child prodigy and defining Classical era genius who transformed public art.

## Main Characteristics
- Exhibited unparalleled melodic fluency masking profound psychological complexity.
- Revolutionized opera by adding deep social commentary and complex characterization.
- Wrote prolifically across all musical genres with absolute technical precision.

## Learning Insights
True genius combines technical perfection with deep understanding of the human condition.

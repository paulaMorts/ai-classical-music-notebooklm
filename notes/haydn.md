# Joseph Haydn

## Historical Context
Classical era pioneer who thrived under the patronage of the Esterházy family.

## Main Characteristics
- Systematized and elevated the symphony and string quartet formats.
- Mastered thematic development by exploring simple melodic motives exaustively.
- Promoted instrumental democracy, giving equal voice to all quartet members.

## Learning Insights
Working within strict structural boundaries can lead to incredible innovation and sophistication.

# Christoph Willibald Gluck

## Historical Context
Mid-18th-century composer who reformed stagnant, overly ornamented Italian opera seria.

## Main Characteristics
- Prioritized dramatic integrity and narrative over virtuosic vocal displays.
- Integrated chorus, dance, and orchestra actively into the dramatic storyline.
- Sought a "beautiful simplicity" to convey deep human emotions.

## Learning Insights
Simplifying art to its core message often creates a deeper emotional impact.

# Vladimir Horowitz

## Historical Context
20th-century legendary Ukrainian-American pianist known for breathtaking virtuosity and emotional depth.

## Main Characteristics
- Delivered highly personal interpretations with massive, highly expressive dynamic contrasts.
- Took a nearly 10-year hiatus to introspectively refine his technique.
- Served as an emotional cultural bridge during the Cold War.

## Learning Insights
Stepping away from pressure to introspectively refine skills yields unstoppable confidence.

# Frédéric Chopin

## Historical Context
Polish Romantic composer who channeled his physical frailty into poetic piano music.

## Main Characteristics
- Mastered extreme dynamic contrasts to compensate for his physical weakness.
- Utilized highly articulated, snake-like sliding hand movements on the keys.
- Composed deeply melancholic and passionate works almost exclusively for piano.

## Learning Insights
Turning physical limitations into unique stylistic strengths creates unmatched artistic identity.

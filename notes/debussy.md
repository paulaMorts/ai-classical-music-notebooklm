# Claude Debussy

## Historical Context
Late 19th-century French modern composer who rejected traditional classical conventions.

## Main Characteristics
- Explored Oriental cultures to create different musical textures and timbres.
- Despised the term "impressionist," preferring his music to be called "different".
- Composed his melancholic masterpiece Clair de Lune facing terminal cancer.


## Learning Insights
Breaking established rules to forge your own path creates a timeless artistic legacy.

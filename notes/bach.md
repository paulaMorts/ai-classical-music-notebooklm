# Johann Sebastian Bach

## Historical Context
Baroque era composer from a deeply religious and musical German family.

## Main Characteristics
- Mastered complex tonal counterpoint and fugues.
- Extremely prolific, creating new works weekly for church masses and cantatas.
- Masterful organist and harpsichordist known for intricate and complex improvisations.

## Learning Insights
Dedication and complexity often gain true recognition long after a creator's lifetime.

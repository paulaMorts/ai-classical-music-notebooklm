# Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky

## Historical Context
19th-century Russian composer whose musical career started late despite family objections.

## Main Characteristics
- Blended Russian folk melodies with structured Western European musical traditions.
- Suffered intense anxiety and agonizing creative processes while composing.
- Created highly emotional symphonies, ballets, and concertos.

## Learning Insights
Great creative achievements often require agonizing persistence and rigorous problem-solving.

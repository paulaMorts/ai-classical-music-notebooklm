# Baroque (c. 1600 – 1750)

## Characteristics
- complex tonal counterpoint 
- continuous bass line

## Main Composers
- Johann Sebastian Bach
- Antonio Vivaldi
- Georg Friedrich Händel
- Domenico Scarlatti
- Jean-Philippe Rameau

## Historical Impact
- Established tonal harmony, standardized orchestral ensembles, and birthed opera and concerto formats.

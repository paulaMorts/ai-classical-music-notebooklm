# Classicism (c. 1750 – 1820)

## Characteristics
- search for balance
- symmetrical forms
- structural clarity

## Main Composers
- Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
- Joseph Haydn 
- Christoph Willibald Gluck
- Ludwig van Beethoven

## Historical Impact
- Reflected Enlightenment ideals of reason, transforming courtly entertainment into public, philosophical art.

# Romanticism (c. 1820 – 1910)

## Characteristics
- extensive melodic lines
- passionate emotional expression


## Main Composers
- Frédéric Chopin
- Felix Mendelssohn
- Robert Schumann, Richard Wagner
- Franz Liszt, Johannes Brahms
- Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky

## Historical Impact
- Broke structural molds to express profound emotion and established musicians as independent artists.
